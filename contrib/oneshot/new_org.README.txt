If your folder structure differs significantly from the structure
above, using new_org.exe is discouraged.

In the notes section of your pages,
<a href="%sm=IMH;s=file.jpg">text</a>  ->  <a href="%sm=IMH;s=file.jpg">
<a href="%sm=SRC;v=file">text</a>      ->  <a href="%sm=SRC;v=file">text</a>
                                       or  <a href="%sm=DOC;s=file.txt">text</a>

images as portraits are searched in documents/portraits
other images (m=IM, m=IMH, m=DOC) are searched in documents/images
or documents.
for text documents (m=SRC, m=DOC&s=file.txt) a search on file_lg.txt
is performed before file.txt (where lg is the current language).

In documents, other sub-folders are allowed.
